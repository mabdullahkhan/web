Engineering Solutions
Beyond Boundaries

I'm M Abdullah Khan, an Electrical & Electronic Engineering student at United International University, Dhaka, specializing in Robotics and Embedded Systems. My work sits at the intersection of ground-level IoT and orbital-scale space technology.

Over the past few years, I've had the privilege of competing on the world stage — placing 7th globally in the 2025 CanSat Competition (organized by NASA and the American Astronautical Society) and 13th globally in the University Rover Challenge 2022, ranked highest in Asia. These aren't just numbers; they're proof that engineering from Bangladesh can compete with the best in the world.

As the National Point of Contact for Bangladesh at the Space Generation Advisory Council (SGAC), I coordinate space advocacy at the national level and represent my country in international forums. I believe space is not a luxury — it's a necessity for humanity's future, and every nation deserves a seat at that table.

Beyond hardware, I'm a published researcher, an IEEE leader, and someone who genuinely loves mentoring and building communities around science and technology. When I'm not designing circuits or debugging telemetry, I'm writing about the ideas that will shape tomorrow.
